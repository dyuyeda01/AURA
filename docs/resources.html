How to Use AURA

AURA provides a unified view of daily cyber risk by fusing exploit intelligence, vulnerability data, and AI-driven context. Each entry reflects the most relevant CVEs with enrichment from public and open-source data feeds.

  • Start with the Top 10 CVEs for immediate threat visibility.
  • Expand any entry to see detailed scoring, trend data, and exploit references.
  • Use the AURA Score to prioritize based on risk and likelihood of exploitation.
  • Track how emerging AI-related vulnerabilities are shaping the cyber landscape.

Data Sources

AURA combines multiple trusted and open data sources to provide context and scoring depth. Each component contributes a different dimension to the overall risk calculation:

AURA Scoring Framework

The AURA Score is a composite index blending technical severity, exploit probability, active exploitation, exposure trends, and AI-related context into a unified 0‚Äì100 risk scale.

Weights are dynamically tuned for balanced technical accuracy and operational relevance:

  • CVSS: 40%
  • EPSS: 20%
  • KEV: 20%
  • Exploit Availability: 10%
  • Trend Awareness: 5%
  • AI Context: 5%
