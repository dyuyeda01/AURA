How to Use AURA

AURA provides a unified view of daily cyber risk by fusing exploit intelligence, vulnerability data, and AI-driven context. Each entry reflects the most relevant CVEs enriched from public and open-source data feeds.

  • Start with the Top 10 CVEs for immediate threat visibility.
  • Expand entries to see detailed scoring, trend data, and exploit references.
  • Use the AURA Score to prioritize based on likelihood and impact.
  • Track how AI-related vulnerabilities influence the modern threat landscape.

AURA Scoring Framework

The AURA Score blends severity, exploitation, and AI context into a unified 0-100 scale:

  • CVSS: 40%
  • EPSS: 20%
  • KEV: 20%
  • Exploit Availability: 10%
  • Trend Awareness: 5%
  • AI Context: 5%
