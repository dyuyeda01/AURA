How to Use AURA

  • Start with the Top 10 to get daily situational awareness.
  • Click a CVE to view scoring breakdown and AI summary.
  • Use Trends to explore vendors, categories, and score movements.
  • Prioritize patching based on AURA Score and context to your environment.

Data Sources

AURA fuses multiple public sources:

  • CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV)
  • NVD (CVSS scores & metadata)
  • FIRST EPSS (exploit probability)
  • Exploit-DB / GitHub (public PoC availability)
  • Cyber advisories & news (RSS)
